Avinashilingam Institute for Home Science and Higher Education for Women, popularly known as Avinshiligam University is a woman deemed to be University in Coimbatore. With a 91-92% placement rate, the university has recently placed 821 women students in companies like Ernst & Young, Cognizant, Maveric, TNQ, Ford, Wipro, Dell, Reliance Communications, Canara Bank, Bosch, CSS, iGATE, Renault Nissan, 2adpro, ugam, Standard Chartered, etc.
Placement Experience : At our university, there is a good placement cell. Students are also getting job offers from HCL, WIPRO, etc, But only a few will get such offers from reputed companies. Almost 90% will get offer letters but due to less pay, many will opt for doing a master's degree. Also, there won't is any placement classes which is a drawback. Internships Opportunities : In our college, to complete the degree, going for on-the-job training i.e. internship is a must. We must go through 2 on job training. One in first-year summer vacation and another in second-year vacation. Those on-the-job training has to be done by the students outside the campus on their own and should get internship certificates.
